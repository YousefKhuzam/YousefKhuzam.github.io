🚨 A New Wave of Degree Mill Scams Targeting Professionals in the GCC 🚨

Recently, a surge of scams targeting professionals and job seekers in the GCC has emerged.

  • Fake LinkedIn profiles pretending to be HR managers.
  • Directing victims to fake educational websites.
  • Collecting personal/financial data for fraud.
  • Charging small fees that add up.
  • Issuing worthless certificates.

How to stay safe: Always verify LinkedIn profiles, avoid suspicious links, and never share sensitive info.
